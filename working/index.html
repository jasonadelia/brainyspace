Bringing coworking to the Brainy Borough

At Brainy Space we are creating a community of independant professionals, entrepreneurs, artists, small business owners, telecommuters and others who are tired of the isolation of the home office, the distractions of the coffee shop, and the commutes to NYC. We believe that building a collaborative work environment will help all Brainy Space members grow, learn and thrive.

What to expect at the Brainy Space

Coworking spaces provide the amenities of a typical office setting. We are just getting started and are looking to the community to determine its exact needs but you can expect:

  • desks with comfortable chairs
  • hi-speed wired and wireless internet
  • kitchen with coffee and tea
  • lounge area
  • meeting rooms
  • print / scan / copy / fax services
  • business address & mail reception
  • white boards
  • tiered membership options:
    • drop-in
    • part time
    • full time
  • event hosting
  • a kick ass community of like-minded pros
