Historic Preservation Scavenger Hunt

June 1, 2014 through June 30, 2014

The Borough’s Historic Preservation Committee is once again hosting a self-paced Scavenger Hunt, with the elements in this year’s contest all visible from the sidewalk at street level along Main Street between Middlesex and Amboy Avenues. Prizes will be awarded in categories and additional runners-up may be chosen, so even if you can’t identify all the photographs, you should still send it in!

Many thanks to the Metuchen Area Chamber of Commerce for their generous support and donation of gift certificates as prizes, and to Ernie Agtarap of Metuchen Living for providing the photographs.
